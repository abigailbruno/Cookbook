Lemon Pasta Recipe

Serving Size: 2

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups of water
  • 3 lemons
  • 3 clove garlic
  • 1/2 cup of lemon zest
  • 1/2 cup of basil
  • 1 box of thin pasta
  • 4 Tbsp of olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp of unsalted butter
  • crushed red pepper flakes

Instructions:

Zest lemons until 1/2 cup of lemon zest is produced. Cut and juice 3 lemons; set aside for now. Next boil 6 cups of water. Then in a hot pan add 4 Tbsp of olive oil, 3 cloves of garlic, and 1 Tbsp of unsalted butter. Once these ingredients have been incorporated add in the box of thin pasta. Immediately begin to ladle in a few cups of the boiling water; repeat process until pasta is cooked. Once the pasta is cooked add in crushed red pepper flakes to taste and the juice of 3 squeezed lemons. Next garnish with 1/2 of basil and 1/2 cup of lemon zest.
