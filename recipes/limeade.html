Limeade Recipe

Serving Size: 2

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups of water
  • 3 limes
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of lime zest

Instructions:

In a large pitcher add 6 cups of water. Add 3 freshly squeezed limes. Next stir in 2 cups of sugar. Add in 1/2 cup of lime zest. Once all ingredients are thoroughly mixed serve over ice in a fancy glass.
